Our Vision

Our vision is simple: to create the most unhinged, unapologetic, and boundary-shattering animated series the screen has ever seen. Operating in the wild-west of web animation means no suits, no censorship, and zero permissions asked. We are here to weaponize taboos, stir up chaos, and make some serious, global noise.

But don't let the madness fool you. Beneath the sci-fi chaos lies a razor-sharp mission: to wage war on modern antisemitism. We aren't here to lecture. Instead, we take historical trauma and current events and flip them into brilliant galactic metaphors that hit you right in basic human logic. We are here to systematically dismantle the cynical excuse that "anti-Zionism is not antisemitism."

We have absolutely no intention of sugarcoating reality or painting Jews as flawless, holy "beacon of light." Our characters are deeply flawed, chaotic, and messy. Just like every other human being in the universe. And that's the whole point. Through the bizarre alien civilizations we build, we hold a mirror up to the world's blatant double standards and put the hypocrisy of antisemitism on blast.

This series is our ultimate statement: We are officially done hiding. We are done apologizing for our identity. Deal with it.

About the Series

After thousands of years of wandering, Earth's Jewish population thought they'd finally found their home. They were wrong.

When Earth goes fully antisemitic (not partially, not subtly, but dramatically and completely), a last-ditch crew boards the Judith, a not-quite-spaceworthy vessel, and sets off into the cosmos in search of a place that will have them.

Spoiler: no one does.

THE JUDITH is a dark, biting, proudly unrepentant animated comedy that treats antisemitism the way Jews have always treated it: as material. Each episode lands the crew on a new planet, facing a new civilization, and a new creative reason to be turned away.

Created with AI animation and old-fashioned human writing, the series pushes the boundaries of what independent animation can be, and what comedy can say.

New Angle Studios

New Angle Studios is an independent animation studio built at the intersection of decades of creative industry experience and next-generation AI production tools. We believe the future of storytelling belongs to those who are unafraid to point the camera somewhere no one else is looking, and to use every tool available to get there. Our work is fast, bold, and uncompromising.
